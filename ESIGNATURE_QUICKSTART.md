### For Users

**How to add a signature to a work log:**

1. Fill out your work log form
2. Scroll to **"Signatures"** section
3. Click **"Add Signature"**
4. Enter your **name** and **role** (optional)
5. **Draw** your signature in the box
6. Click **"Save Signature"**
7. **Submit** the work log

Done! Your signature is now saved with the work log and will appear in PDF exports.

## Keyboard Shortcuts

- **Clear signature:** Click "Clear" button
- **Remove signature:** Click X on signature card
- **Add another:** Click "Add Signature" again

---

## Mobile/Tablet Usage

- Use your **finger** or **stylus**
- Draw in the **white box**
- Zoom if needed for precision
- Hold device **steady** while signing

---

## PDF Export

Your signatures will automatically appear in the PDF when you click "Export"

**Each signature shows:**
- Your name
- Your role
- Date and time signed
- Your signature image
